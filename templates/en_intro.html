Negahe-nou research institute has been established at 2013, as a non-governmental center, to support the elites and young individuals or groups who have potential power to progress in various economical and industrial fields. Financial sources and the management system of institute assist the young elites by different approaches:

  • Appreciating their contribution in institute current projects.
  • Supporting them to complete their own projects individually or in scientific clusters.
  • Making the necessary infrastructures for their research or production.
  • Supporting the new ideas and plans to lead to product and/or wealth.

Negahe-nou mostly supports and invests in projects that include an original approach or a novel technology.

Registration

Students and elites need to register first to use the website facilities. Registration is possible in two ways:

Individual registration: In this case, the researcher has a project and wishes to work individually or he likes to contribute in institute current project.

Cluster registration: In this case, a group of elites, students or researchers would like to register a common project and work as a team. The one who registers the group is identified as the head. Anyhow, names and email addresses of other members are needed for registration as well.

Recording and presenting the proposal

The user should record his/her project after individual or cluster registration. There is a standard form which should be filled out and sent back to the institute. The following chart shows the flow work:

Following factors and conditions are considered to review the proposal:

  1. Proposal has to include a new characteristic which is not much considered yet.
  2. In addition to being new, the proposed idea has to result in qualitative and quantitative improvement in its field. In other words it should have considerable advantage over probable or present solutions.
  3. If the proposal is theoretical, it should have the capacity to be used in industry and production.
  4. All the rights are reserved for the proposal owner.

These are just main and initial criteria to reject or accept a proposal and more careful process will occur after initial acceptance.

Judgment

After initial decision explained in the previous part and before supportive procedure the proposal is sent to referees who are specialists in the corresponding field of research.
